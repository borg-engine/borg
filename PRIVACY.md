# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 15, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

## Introduction

Borg ("we", "our", "us") is an AI agent orchestrator. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Borg mobile application and related services.

## Data We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account credentials**: Server URL, authentication tokens, and API keys required to connect to your Borg instance.
- **Messages and commands**: Text input you send through the chat interface to interact with AI agents.
- **Files and attachments**: Images or documents you upload for processing by agents.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and debugging.
- **Usage data**: Feature usage patterns and interaction timestamps to improve the application.
- **Push notification tokens**: Device tokens required to deliver notifications.

## How We Use Your Data

- To connect you to your self-hosted or managed Borg instance and relay messages to AI agents.
- To deliver push notifications for task updates and agent responses.
- To improve app stability and performance through anonymized usage analytics.
- To provide customer support when requested.

## Third-Party Services

The app may interact with the following third-party services:

- **Expo / React Native**: Application framework and push notification delivery.
- **Your configured Borg server**: All chat messages and task data are sent to the server URL you configure. We do not control data handling on self-hosted instances.
- **AI providers** (as configured on your server): Claude (Anthropic), Ollama, or other LLM providers your Borg instance connects to.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## Data Storage and Retention

- **Local data**: Authentication tokens and app preferences are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage (Expo SecureStore).
- **Server data**: Messages, tasks, and agent interactions are stored on your configured Borg server instance. Retention is governed by your server's configuration.
- **We do not store** your messages or data on any intermediary servers. The app communicates directly with your Borg instance.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encrypted local storage for sensitive credentials.
- HTTPS for all network communication.
- No intermediary data collection or logging servers.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data stored on your Borg server instance.
- **Delete** your local app data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the application.
- **Delete** your server-side data through your Borg instance's administration interface.
- **Opt out** of push notifications through your device settings.

## Children's Privacy

The Borg application is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the application or our website.
